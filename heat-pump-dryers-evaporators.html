Frequently Asked Questions

What is a heat pump dryer or evaporator and how does it work?

Heat pump dryers and evaporators use a closed-loop refrigerant cycle to remove moisture or concentrate liquids while recovering and reusing heat.

Are heat pump dryers and evaporators energy efficient?

Yes. By recycling thermal energy within the system, heat pump dryers and evaporators can reduce energy consumption by up to 50‚Äì70% compared to traditional steam or electric heating systems.

Can they be used for sensitive products or low-temperature drying?

Absolutely. Heat pump dryers and evaporators can operate at lower and controlled temperatures, making them ideal for heat-sensitive products such as food, pharmaceuticals, and specialty chemicals.

What is the typical lifespan of these systems?

With proper operation and regular maintenance, heat pump dryers and evaporators can last 15 years or more, offering reliable performance and consistent product quality over time.
