Frequently Asked Questions

What is a heat pump and how does it work?

Heat pumps transfer heat from one place to another using a refrigerant cycle. They can be used for both heating and cooling.

Are heat pumps energy efficient?

Yes, heat pumps are more energy efficient compared to traditional heating methods because they move heat rather than generate it.

Can heat pumps be used in cold climates?

Modern heat pumps can work efficiently in colder climates with improved technology such as variable speed compressors.
